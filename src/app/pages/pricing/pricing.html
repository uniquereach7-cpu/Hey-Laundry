Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free pickup and delivery?

Yes, we offer complimentary doorstep pickup and delivery across all major areas in Hyderabad for orders above ₹500.

What is your turnaround time?

Standard wash & fold orders are returned within 24-48 hours. We also offer an express same-day service for an additional charge.

Do you mix clothes with other customers?

Absolutely not. We process every customer's laundry individually in dedicated machines to maintain our strict hygiene standards.

What happens if it rains during delivery?

Your garments are protected in our specialized waterproof delivery bags ensuring they arrive at your door in pristine condition regardless of weather.
