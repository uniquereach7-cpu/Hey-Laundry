Return & Cancellation Policy

RAPS FABRIC LABS Private Limited (Brand: Hey! Laundry & Dry Cleaning)
Effective Date: March 1st, 2026

1. Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be cancelled before pickup without any charges.
  • Once the order has been picked up, cancellation may not be possible as the cleaning process may have already begun.

For cancellation requests, please contact us at the earliest via phone or WhatsApp.

2. Refund Policy

Refunds may be issued under the following circumstances:

  • Service not delivered as committed
  • Garment lost or significantly damaged during processing
  • Incorrect billing or duplicate payment

All refund requests will be reviewed by our team before approval.

3. Refund Process

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days
  • Refunds will be issued to the original mode of payment
  • In certain cases, refunds may be provided as store credit (with customer consent)

4. Service Issues & Reprocessing

If you are not satisfied with the quality of service:

  • You may request a free re-cleaning within 24 hours of delivery
  • The garment must be unused after delivery for reprocessing eligibility

5. Limitations of Liability

While we take utmost care in handling garments:

  • Compensation for lost or damaged items will be limited to a reasonable amount, based on garment condition, age, and processing risk
  • We are not liable for:
    • Pre-existing damage
    • Color fading or shrinkage due to fabric nature
    • Items left in pockets
